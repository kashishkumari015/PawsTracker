About Pawstrack

Pawstrack is an advanced livestock health monitoring system that leverages AI, IoT, and wearable technology to provide real-time health tracking, disease detection, and predictive analytics for livestock. Based in the heart of rural innovation, we collaborate with veterinarians, farmers, and tech pioneers to ensure Pawstrack meets real-world needs. Let's work together to keep your livestock thriving! Founded by a team of tech enthusiasts and agricultural experts, we’re passionate about improving livestock welfare with years of experience in IoT and animal science.

Why Pawstrack?

Traditional livestock monitoring relies on manual observation—time-consuming and often too late to catch diseases early. Pawstrack offers a smarter, automated solution, empowering farmers and veterinarians with data-driven decisions.

Key Benefits

  • Real-Time Monitoring: Continuous tracking of animal health and activity.
  • Early Disease Detection: AI-based analysis spots issues before they worsen.
  • Improved Productivity: Optimize farm operations with data insights.
  • Cost-Effective: Cut veterinary costs and prevent livestock loss.
  • Animal Welfare: Keep animals healthy and stress-free.
